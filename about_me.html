FOUNDER

Hello, Im Christian Cleveland. I was born and raised in California and got to see amazing wilderness areas and grew up exploring Southern California and all of its hidden gems. I grew up wondering what happens to the wilderness and to the earth when humans do something harmful and what happens when humans spread their reach globally. The answer is that fragile ecoystems collapse and species become extinct and the earth gets exploited. It is my passion to spread awareness about the delicate state in which California is in and to rally for environmental conservation and sustainability.
